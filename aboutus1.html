About Us

Saarrthi design Pvt. Ltd. Interior Design is a full-service interior design company located in the Seattle area since 2019, specializing in both residential and commercial design. Whether you need a quick refresh of furniture and paint colors, or an extensive whole-house renovation, we can provide the expertise to make sure the project runs smoothly and gives you the best results possible.

OUR MISSION

we are a small design firm, we are extremely flexible and nimble. We have relationships with many local craftsmen and subcontractors, so you can be assured that your project is completed with the highest standards. Or, if you prefer, you can hire your own contractors & We'll Work With Them Throughout The remodeling Process.

You can contact us for support by telephone, E-Mail, or mobile (including text/SMS messaging/Whatsapp).We will always reply quickly, efficiently and will solve your query in a constructive, timely and professional manner.
